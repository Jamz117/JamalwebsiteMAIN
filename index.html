About Us

At London Football Matches, we make booking pitch time simple, fast, and reliable. Whether you’re organising a training session, a friendly 5-a-side, or a full club fixture, our platform connects you with top venues across the city, real-time availability, and secure checkout all in one place.

Why Choose Us?

  • Extensive venue network across the city
  • Real-time availability and instant booking
  • Secure, PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Dedicated customer support and dispute handling
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Team and group booking tools with split payments
  • Verified venue quality and up-to-date photos
  • Flexible cancellation and rescheduling policies

Our Services

We provide tailored booking and venue management solutions for teams, clubs, and individual players — fast search, secure checkout, and responsive support to make organising matches simple.

Booking Management

Advanced booking tools for single and recurring matches, with calendar syncing, reminders, and easy rescheduling.

Payment Processing

Secure, PCI-compliant payments with saved cards, invoices, and streamlined refund handling for hassle-free transactions.

Venue Profiles

Detailed venue listings with photos, facility details, and user reviews to help you choose the right pitch for your needs.

Team Coordination

Invite teammates, manage rosters, and split payments with integrated communication tools for smooth team organisation.

Event Promotion

Promote matches and events to local players and teams to help you fill slots quickly and increase community engagement.

Analytics & Reporting

Insights into bookings, attendance, and revenue to help venues and organisers make data-driven decisions.
